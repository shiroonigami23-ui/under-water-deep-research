# Artificial Deep-Water Structures

Status date: April 19, 2026.

## Structure Categories

- Observation infrastructure:
  - Cabled observatories, moorings, benthic landers, fixed camera nodes.
- Industrial infrastructure:
  - Subsea cables, pipelines, deep foundations, mining prototypes.
- Energy systems:
  - Offshore deepwater platforms, deep mooring arrays, subsea power systems.
- Research platforms:
  - ROV and AUV docking sites, long-term instrumentation frames.

## Scientific Concerns

- Local habitat disruption and sediment plume effects.
- Noise, vibration, and electromagnetic impacts.
- Biofouling and species colonization dynamics.
- Failure modes under pressure, corrosion, fatigue, and currents.

## Governance and Policy-Relevant Stream

- ISA mining code negotiations (ongoing intersessional process after 2025 session milestones) are central for deep-seabed industrial structure deployment in international waters.
