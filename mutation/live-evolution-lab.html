Algorithm (conceptual) 1. Read environment E in [0,1]. 2. Blend target phenotype T = lerp(shallowGenome, deepGenome, E). 3. Apply mutation pulse M at random intervals: genome = genome + N(0, sigma) + rare jump. 4. Selection pull: genome = genome + alpha * (T - genome). 5. Compute fitness = 1 - normalized_distance(genome, T). 6. Render fish body, eye, fin, pigment, and biolum traits.
